EasyRide has a dedicated staff that recruits, trains, and tends to our herd of Cars. We take great pride in the quality of Cars and rides that we provide to our customers, and our staff exercises the utmost care in vetting the cars that join our herd.

Every car goes through a rigorous due diligence process where we perform background checks, flying exams, and several rounds of interviews. Car accepted to EasyRide are then treated to the best care and maintenance possible. We provide them excellent benefits, health care, and employee perks. This is part of our company philosophy in which Comfortable Cars lead to happy customers.
